03

What actually
helped me improve.

Grounded in real frustration, not perfect hindsight. Advice lands better when it's honest about the struggle.

01

Use AI as a tutor

Ask why code works, not how to paste a fix. Speed matters, but understanding is what compounds over months.

02

Ship tiny projects

A small working build teaches more than hours of passive tutorial watching. Completion beats perfection.

03

Keep your old code

Revisiting rough work is one of the clearest ways to measure real progress. Don't delete it.

04

Read the docs early

Ten minutes in the docs saves hours of confused debugging and wrong assumptions.
